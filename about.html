About Kerala Unani Wellness Clinic

Where Kerala's healing tradition meets the UK

✦

Kerala Unani Wellness Clinic was founded with a singular purpose: to bring the authentic, evidence-informed wisdom of Unani medicine to patients in the United Kingdom who are seeking alternatives to symptom-only approaches.

Practising from Bliss Gx in Gerrards Cross, Buckinghamshire, the clinic provides a serene, professional environment where ancient healing traditions are delivered with modern clinical rigour.

Our name reflects our roots. Kerala — the land known for its centuries-long tradition of holistic medicine. Unani — the ancient system of healing originating in Greece and refined through centuries of Arabic and South Asian medical scholarship.

Every consultation, every treatment, every interaction is guided by one belief: that true healing begins when we understand the whole person — not just the presenting complaint.
